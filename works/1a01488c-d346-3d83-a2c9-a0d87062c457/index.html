Εφουμέρναμ' ένα βράδυ

The song Efoumernam’ ena vrady is the first song that Markos Vamvakaris recorded. It was first released in 1932, on a 72 RPM record by Columbia Records, with catalog number DG 326. On the B side of the same record was the instrumental improvisation Taxim serf. On the label printed on side A of the 1932 record, the song is credited as Efoumaram’ ena vrady (“efoumaram’” instead of “efoumernam’“), using a different tense of the verb “foumaro”.

The song has been later recorded by artists including Giorgos Ntalaras, Stelios Vamvakaris, Apostolos Nikolaidis, and Dimitris Mystakidis.
