100 δίσκοι και η ιστορία τους

The booklet 100 δίσκοι και η ιστορία τους (translation: 100 records and their story) was prepared by the producers and editors of the Μελωδία (translation: Melody) radio station and distributed with an issue of newspaper Καθημερινή (translation: The Daily). It contains 100 short chapters, each telling the story behind the creation of a Greek record.
