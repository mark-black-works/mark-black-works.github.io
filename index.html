Book: 100 diskoi kai i istoria tous

The booklet 100 δίσκοι και η ιστορία τους (translation: 100 records and their story) was prepared by the producers and editors of the Μελωδία (translation: Melody) radio station and distributed with the issue of newspaper Καθημερινή (translation: The Daily) of 2003-06-29. It contains 100 short chapters, each telling the story behind the creation of a Greek record.

I’ve made a list of the 100 records referenced in this booklet, see: 100 δίσκοι και η ιστορία τους.

Published: 2020-03-21

TV Series: To minore tis avgis

TV series “Το μινόρε της αυγής” (“To minore tis avgis”) ran for 2 seasons in 1983 and 1984. Most of the main characters in the series are musicians, and so there is a lot of music in every episode. All episodes are available to watch at the ERT Archive. I have compiled a list of episodes with links to watch them at Το μινόρε της αυγής. Further, in the page for every episode I have noted the songs that play in that episode.

For some more information and cast details see To minore tis avgis on IMDB.

Published: 2020-03-16
