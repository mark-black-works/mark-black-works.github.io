Το μινόρε της αυγής S02E03

This is the third episode in the second season of TV series Το μινόρε της αυγής. In addition to the songs listed below, there is an instrumental improvisation playing in the beginning of the episode, during the narration of the previous episode.

This episode is available to watch at ERT Archive.
