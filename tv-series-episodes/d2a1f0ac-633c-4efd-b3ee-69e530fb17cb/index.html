Το μινόρε της αυγής S01E01

This episode is available to watch at ERT Archive.

Works

Songs played in this episode:

#WorkNote
1 Το μινόρε της αυγής Instrumental during opening credits and first few scenes. This is the only episode during which the titular song can be heard in its entirety, in the instrumental version.
2 Ο ψαράς μέσα στη χώρα This is the first song featured in the series, besides the series theme. Played and sang by the composer and lyricist, Yorgos Mouflouzelis.
3 Το μινόρε της αυγής Second time this song is played in this episode. This time in a scene in which the main characters serenade a girl, under her window.
4 Κάτω στα λεμονάδικα (Οι λαχανάδες) Cover played with 2 bouzouki and vocals.
5 Μικρός αρραβωνιάστηκα Played during a scene after a wedding, with actor Ilias Logothetis dancing.
6 Μικρός αρραβωνιάστηκα Second time this song plays during this episode, this time as instrumental over the closing credits.
