Το μινόρε της αυγής

Το μινόρε της αυγής (transliterated: To minore tis avgis, translated: Minor [song] for dawn) was a Greek TV series that aired in 1983 and 1984.

Music

The series shares the same title with the song Το μινόρε της αυγής by Spyros Peristeris and Minos Matsas, and the song features prominently in the series, most frequently in an instrumental cover during the opening and closing credits of each episode, and also once in its original recording and a few more times in other covers.

As the main characters are musicians, a lot more Greek songs and a few instrumentals are played throughout the series.
