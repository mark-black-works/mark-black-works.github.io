Παρών

The record Παρών (transliterated: Paron, translated: Present, as in being here rather than relating to now) was first released in 1977 in vinyl, then in 1993 in CD, and again in 2007. The 1977 release contained 12 tracks, as did the 1993 one. The 2007 release contains 24 tracks, with the last 12 being instrumental arrangements of the songs.
